互動式英語自我修正指南

Interactive English Self-Correction Guide

歡迎！Welcome!

本應用程式旨在提供一個互動的方式來瀏覽「英語自我修正指南」。您可以從左側的選單中選擇一個修正點或翻譯範例進行學習。每個修正點都包含原文、改進後的句子、詳細的改進原因（中英雙語）以及更多實用範例。翻譯範例則提供中文表述及其對應的英文翻譯。

This application is designed to provide an interactive way to explore the "English Self-Correction Guide." You can select a correction point or a translation example from the menu on the left to study. Each correction point includes the original sentence, improved versions, detailed reasons for improvement (in both English and Chinese), and further practical examples. Translation examples provide Chinese expressions and their corresponding English translations.
